How do I play?

So, how do you play? It's quite simple, just open up minecraft on your PC with any version that works on hypixel (1.8.9 is recommended!).
Once you've opend up the game, join the Hypixel server:

Click the housing gamemode (dark wooden door) in the game menu. Go to "browse housings" and search for "puffenminecraft" or "bonkersturnip".
Here you will find our housings, simply click the one you want to join and you're in!
